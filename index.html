Journey

"TO GET RID OF THIS 9-5 ROUTINE LIFE" as someone has rightly said; "The routine is Enemy of Time" and he was right. I don't want to kill my time who know which date of calendar might be last on earth.So, exploring new places, people, their culture and cuisines are something that always inspire me to pack my rucksack and to push my limit a little further.This feeling of getting lost in wilderness,in the lap of mighty mountains and mesmerising mother nature where you feel alone in the world of more than billions of people can't be explain in few word, you need to get out of your comfort zone to have that thrill.

~Shlok Pandey
